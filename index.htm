Taedong International Travel Solutions Limited

大同國際旅遊諮詢有限公司，專司旅遊諮詢，秉持專業，恪守誠信。

Taedong International Travel Solutions Ltd. is a travel consulting company providing itinerary planning and coordination services for independent travelers.

服務 / Services

  • 自由行旅客旅遊諮詢 / Travel consulting for independent travelers
  • 客製化行程規劃 / Custom itinerary planning
  • 協助對接第三方旅遊服務供應商 / Coordination of travel arrangements via third-party providers
  • 一般旅遊建議與支援 / General travel advice and support

運作方式 / How It Works

  1. 客戶提交旅遊需求 / Client submits travel inquiry
  2. 提供諮詢與建議 / We provide consultation and recommendations
  3. 設計專屬行程 / We design a custom itinerary
  4. 由第三方供應商完成預訂與履約 / Third-party providers handle bookings and fulfillment

服務示例 / Sample Service

例子：客戶提出七天行程需求，我們提供客製化建議，包括路線、酒店與旅遊活動。所有預訂由第三方旅遊供應商直接完成。

Example: A client requests a 7-day itinerary. We provide a customized plan including routes, hotels, and tours. All bookings are completed directly with third-party travel operators.
