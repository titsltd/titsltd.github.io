Taedong International Travel Solutions Limited

大同國際旅遊諮詢有限公司，專司旅遊諮詢，秉持專業，恪守誠信。

Taedong International Travel Solutions Ltd. is a travel consulting company providing itinerary planning and coordination services for independent travelers.

Services / 服務

  • Travel consulting for independent travelers / 自由行旅客旅遊諮詢
  • Custom itinerary planning / 客製化行程規劃
  • Coordination of travel arrangements via third-party providers / 協助對接第三方旅遊服務供應商
  • General travel advice and support / 一般旅遊建議與支援

How It Works / 運作方式

  1. Client submits travel inquiry / 客戶提交旅遊需求
  2. We provide consultation and recommendations / 提供諮詢與建議
  3. We design a custom itinerary / 設計專屬行程
  4. Third-party providers handle bookings and fulfillment / 由第三方供應商完成預訂與履約

Sample Service / 服務示例

Example / 例子：A client requests a 7-day itinerary. We provide a customized plan including routes, hotels, and tours. All bookings are completed directly with third-party travel operators.

客戶提出七天行程需求，我們提供客製化建議，包括路線、酒店與旅遊活動。所有預訂由第三方旅遊供應商直接完成。

Refund & Cancellation Policy / 退款與取消政策

Consulting fees may be refundable prior to service delivery. Once itinerary planning has begun, fees are generally non-refundable.

諮詢費用在服務開始前可依情況退款；一旦開始行程規劃，費用通常不予退還。
